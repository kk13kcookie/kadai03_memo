Senior Fullstack Engineer

Tokyo

Fullstack React Mango DB Node JS Type Script

Job Description

The Senior Full Stack Engineer will contribute to the design, development, and maintenance of our web applications and services. This role involves working across the entire technology stack, from front-end user interfaces to back-end services and databases. Based in our Tokyo office, the ideal candidate will collaborate with cross-functional teams to deliver high-quality software solutions that meet business objectives and user needs.

About the role:

  • Design, develop, and maintain scalable web applications using modern technologies
  • Build responsive and intuitive user interfaces with attention to user experience
  • Develop robust back-end services and APIs that support our products
  • Implement database designs and optimize queries for performance
  • Participate in code reviews to ensure code quality and knowledge sharing
  • Troubleshoot and resolve complex technical issues across the stack
  • Collaborate with product managers, designers, and other engineers
  • Mentor junior developers and contribute to team growth
  • Ensure application security and data protection
  • Participate in agile development processes including planning, estimation, and retrospectives

About you:

You’ll thrive as a Senior Fullstack Engineer if you:

technical Skills

  • 5+ years of experience in full stack web development
  • Strong proficiency in JavaScript/TypeScript and at least one modern front-end framework (React, Angular, or Vue.js)
  • Experience with back-end technologies such as Node.js, Python, Java, or Ruby on Rails
  • Knowledge of SQL and NoSQL databases (PostgreSQL, MySQL, MongoDB)
  • Familiarity with CI/CD pipelines and DevOps practices
  • Understanding of RESTful APIs and microservices architecture
  • Experience with cloud platforms (AWS, GCP, or Azure)
  • Knowledge of version control systems, primarily Git

Qualification

  • Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Engineering, or related technical field (or equivalent practical experience)
  • Experience working in an agile development environment
  • Strong problem-solving skills and attention to detail
  • Excellent communication skills in English (Japanese language skills are a plus)
  • Ability to work independently and as part of a team
  • Experience with cross-cultural teams is advantageous

Preferred Skills

  • Experience with mobile-responsive design and development
  • Familiarity with containerization technologies like Docker and Kubernetes
  • Understanding of UI/UX design principles
  • Experience with test-driven development methodologies
  • Knowledge of cybersecurity best practices
  • Previous experience in a Japanese work environment or multinational company
